About Sus Fort Comics

Get to know the story behind our club and what drives us.

Our Story

Sus Fort Comics, Inc. began as a small gathering of comic enthusiasts in West Chester, Pennsylvania. What started out as casual meet‑ups at the local library quickly grew into a thriving club with members of all ages. We’ve hosted book swaps, artist talks and themed nights exploring everything from Golden Age classics to modern graphic novels.

Over the years, our passion for comics has fueled a desire to build a supportive community. Today, we proudly operate under the name “Sus Fort Comics” (inspired by our motto “suspend disbelief, fortify imagination”). We continue to welcome new readers and seasoned collectors alike.

Our Mission

The goal of Sus Fort Comics, Inc. is to sell our comics and merchandise while bringing humor to our community. We aim to:

  • Share and sell our original comics and cool merch with a healthy dose of humor.
  • Create an inclusive space where readers of all backgrounds feel welcome.
  • Promote literacy and appreciation for visual storytelling.
  • Support local comic book shops, creators and events.
  • Encourage artistic expression and collaboration among members.
