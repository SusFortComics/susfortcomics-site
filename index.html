About Us

Sus Fort Comics, Inc. is a West Chester–based comic book club founded by a group of friends who love graphic stories. Our mission is to sell our original comics and merch, build a vibrant community where fans of all ages can share their favorite titles and keep things funny. Whether you’re into superhero sagas, indie graphic novels or manga, you’ll find fellow enthusiasts here.
